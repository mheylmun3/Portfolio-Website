Recipes Website

This project is still undergoing development, but was inspired by my girlfriend's passion for cooking. She keeps a Google Doc filled with her favorite recipes, each including detailed instructions, ingredient lists, and even a grocery list for easy shopping. The goal of this project is to bring that document to life in the form of a website where she can organize, browse, and edit her recipes with ease.

Additionally, this project will add functionality for her to create new recipes directly from the website, making it a complete and interactive cooking resource tailored to her needs.
