Recipes Website

This project is still undergoing development, but was inspired by my girlfriend's passion for cooking. She keeps a Google Doc filled with her favorite recipes, each including detailed instructions, ingredient lists, and a grocery list for easy shopping. The goal of this project is to bring that document to life in the form of a website where she can browse her recipes and update the shopping list easily from anywhere.

Additionally, this project will add functionality for her to create new recipes directly from the website, making it a complete and interactive cooking resource tailored to her needs.
